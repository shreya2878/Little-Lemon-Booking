Little Lemon

Chicago

Little Lemon was founded by two Italian-Greek chefs and long-time friends, Mario and Adrian, who immigrated to Chicago to share their family culinary secrets.

With over two decades of combined kitchen experience, they blend traditional olive oils, fresh seafood, and Mediterranean spices with modern cooking techniques, creating an unforgettable bistro dining experience right in the heart of the Midwest.
